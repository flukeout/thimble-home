Keep Calm Poster Tutorial

Hello! Follow the steps in this tutorial to learn how to change the words, colors and images in the Keep Calm poster.

  1. Change the words of the poster

    Go to line 18 of index.html and change the text to something new that you've brainstormed. You will see your changes in the preview panel. HTML

    Tip: The <br /> tag represents a line break. Unlike most tags, <br /> tags do not need a corresponding "closing" tag.

  2. Change the background color

    Use the file tree on the left side of the screen to navigate to the file called style.css

    Go to line 2 to change the background-color property. First, just replace darkred with a different color, like yellowgreen. Be sure not to have spaces between the words that describe your color.

  3. Change the Thimble image

    This project comes with two built-in image options — the crown and a thimble. You can see the thimble by clicking on thimble.svg in the file tree.

    1. Open the index.html file.
    2. On line 14 you can see the where the crown image is inserted.
    3. Delete crown.svg and type thimble.svg.
  4. Share your meme

    When you're ready to share your meme, click the Publish button in the top righthand corner. Give your project a description and then click the green Publish button. You'll see a url for your project. Send the url to your family and friends, or post it on Facebook or Twitter using the hashtag #teachtheweb.

You're done! Thank you for completing the tutorial, we hope you learned a lot. If you have questions, do this and that.
